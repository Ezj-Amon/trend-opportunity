# Abstention rules

Submit `insufficient_evidence` when the Bundle is not ready, citations are missing, sources conflict without resolution, or only titles/snippets are available.

Submit `abstained` when evidence is ready but the event is a short-lived incident, weakly related to consumer behavior, high-risk/public-interest sensitive, software/service-only, or lacks a plausible durable change within product lead time.

Submit `worth_following` only when evidence supports a durable change, affected users, new scenarios, unmet needs, and relevant physical-product categories. This is still not market validation.

A model may draft only the cited OpportunityAssessment. It must not approve its own draft, create an OpportunitySignal, or force a product output when the correct result is abstention.

Never commercialize tragedy, crime, injury, unauthorized IP/event merchandise, medical efficacy, dangerous goods, or access-control circumvention.
